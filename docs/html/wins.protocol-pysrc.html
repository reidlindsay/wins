 4  Package to implement base classes for protocols. 
 5   
 6  Most protocols should include four ports, namely: 
 7   
 8      1. "TXU" - sends received packets to upstream element. 
 9      #. "RXU" - receives traffic from an upstream element. 
10      #. "TXD" - sends traffic to a downstream element. 
11      #. "RXD" - receives traffic from a downstream element. 
12   
13  Maintaining this convention will make accessing `Port` configurations for 
14  various protocols more straight forward. Some protocols may strictly enforce 
15  all or part of this convention.
